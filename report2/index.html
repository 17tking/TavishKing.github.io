Product Affinity

❓ The Stakeholder's Question: Are there any product combinations we should be promoting together because customers often buy them at the same time on our website?

❗ The Answer: Some home decor categories are selling together, but overall product affinity is weak. Most category pairings show low or negative Lift values. This means customers aren't naturally bundling items.

👉 Shoppers aren't branching out! There is a need to add/improve our site's recommendation engine (e.g. "Customers who bought X, also buy Y") to guide smarter cross-category purchases.

👉 Elevate the online shopping experience! To boost affinity, consider promoting trending combinations on the homepage, offer seasonal bundles, or highlight frequently paired items during checkout.

The Stats:
  • Product affinity is generally low. Only 785 of 99,439 distinct orders had multi-category purchases (0.8%)
  • bed_bath_table and home_comfort have a positive Lift association (Lift = 1.146). Meaning that when a customer buys bed_bath_table products, they are 14.6% more likely to buy home_comfort products
  • The 10 most popular individual categories make up 62.7% of order purchases. Uncover trending cross-category purchases among these categories (see "Top Individual Categories" plot above)
