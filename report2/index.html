KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

I was asked to analyze 6 key areas of our e-commerce performance to help different teams make informed decisions. Below are the KPIs I explored

Product Affinity

Identify which product categories are frequently purchased together to inform cross-sell strategies and enhance the recommendation engine.

Product Reviews

Analyze review volume and average ratings to determine which products and categories are resonating with customers, and where improvements may be needed.

Customer Retention

Measure repeat purchase behavior and time between orders to help assess the effectiveness of retention campaigns.

Sales by Location

Examine geographic performance trends to support regional ad targeting strategies.

Daily Online Orders

Evaluate day-to-day sales activity to detect seasonality and optimize website performance.

Average Order Value

Analyze how much revenue is generated per transaction to identify trends in customer purchasing behavior.
